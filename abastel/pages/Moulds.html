Core Service

Injection Moulding

High-volume injection moulding using PP, ABS, HDPE, LDPE, Nylon, and engineering polymers. Single-cavity to multi-cavity tooling available.

Core Service

CNC Machining

Multi-axis CNC machining of plastic and metal components to tight tolerances. Ideal for prototypes, jigs, fixtures, and low-to-mid volume runs.

In-House

Custom Mould Fabrication

We design and fabricate production moulds in-house — reducing lead times and giving you direct control over tool quality and lifetime.

What We Make

Types of components we produce.

Large Structural Components

Crates, enclosures, trays, and industrial housings moulded at high tonnage for structural applications across telecom, electrical, and industrial sectors.

Precision Moulded Parts

Close-tolerance components with complex geometries — connector bodies, housings, brackets, and sub-assembly parts built to customer drawings.

Machined Components

Turned and milled parts from plastic rod, sheet, and block — ideal for tight-tolerance, low-volume production where tooling investment isn't justified.

Production Tooling & Moulds

P20, H13, and aluminium moulds fabricated in-house or via certified partners. Tool maintenance and repair also available for existing tooling.
