Products

What we manufacture & supply.

Fiber Patch Cords

Single-mode and multimode patch cords in SC, LC, FC, ST, and MTP/MPO connectors. Manufactured in-house for consistent quality.

Optical Fibre Joint Closures

Dome and horizontal joint closures for aerial and underground fiber splicing. IP-rated and built for Indian field conditions.

Fiber Termination Units

Wall-mount and rack-mount FTBs in 12, 24, 48, and 96-core configurations. Ready for LAN and FTTX deployment.

Fiber Optic Cables

Indoor, outdoor, armoured, and ADSS fiber cables. Available in 2-core to 288-core configurations.

Splitters & Adapters

PLC splitters, fiber adapters, and connectors for all standard interfaces. APC and UPC variants in stock.

CATV Accessories

Full range of cable TV accessories including splitters, taps, amplifiers, and connectors for HFC networks.
