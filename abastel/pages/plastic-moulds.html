Overview

Engineered for your exact specification.

Our plastic moulding and machining vertical delivers precision-engineered components for customers across industries — from telecom enclosures to industrial hardware.

With a well-equipped manufacturing setup in Delhi and an experienced team, we handle everything from prototype mould development to high-volume production runs. Whether you need injection-moulded enclosures or CNC-machined metal and plastic components, we operate to tight tolerances and fast lead times.

Services

What we offer.

Injection Moulding

High-pressure injection moulding for ABS, PP, PC, Nylon, and other engineering polymers. Prototype to mass production.

Mould Design & Development

Custom mould design in-house. We take your 2D drawing or 3D model and develop production-ready tooling.

CNC Machining

3-axis and multi-axis CNC machining for plastic and metal components. Tight tolerances, smooth surface finishes.

Custom Enclosures

OEM enclosures for electrical, telecom, and electronic applications. Designed and manufactured to your specs.

Surface Finishing

Painting, texturing, laser marking, and assembly services. We deliver ready-to-use components, not just raw parts.

Quality Inspection

Dimensional inspection using digital gauges and CMM for critical components. Every batch certified before shipping.
