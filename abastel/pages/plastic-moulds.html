Plastic Granules

Manufacturing Through Recycling.

Abastel LLP specialises in manufacturing high-quality plastic granules through advanced recycling and processing of plastic waste — converting post-industrial and post-consumer materials into premium-grade recycled granules.

We help industries reduce environmental impact while ensuring consistent product quality. Our granules are suitable for injection moulding, extrusion, packaging, automotive components, household products, and industrial manufacturing.

Our Process

Waste to value, step by step.

Step 01

Waste Collection & Sorting

Waste is collected and sorted by type — PP, ABS, HDPE, and LDPE — using separate bins. Proper sorting is the foundation of high-quality recycled output.

Step 02

Washing & Grinding

Efficient washing removes contaminants while precision grinding reduces plastic into consistent flakes — clean and ready for melting and extrusion.

Step 03

Melting, Extrusion & Granules

Cleaned plastic is fed into the extruder, melted, shaped through a die, and precision-cut into uniform granules ready for industrial applications.
