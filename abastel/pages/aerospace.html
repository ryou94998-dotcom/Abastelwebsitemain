Overview

Our most demanding vertical.

Abastel's aerospace vertical operates to the highest standards we set across any of our manufacturing lines. Components are designed, machined, and inspected to aerospace-grade tolerances with full material traceability.

We supply precision-machined metal and composite components to aerospace sub-assembly manufacturers and defence system integrators. Our team works to customer-supplied drawings and manages everything from material procurement through final inspection and documentation. Every component ships with a full quality record.
