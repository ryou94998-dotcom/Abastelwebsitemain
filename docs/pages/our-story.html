Where It Began

2019 — A company with something to prove.

Abastel was founded in 2019 in Delhi with one conviction: that Indian manufacturers could deliver world-class quality at Indian prices — without compromise.

Starting with fiber optic products for FTTX and CATV networks, we built our manufacturing facility from the ground up. The state-of-the-art plant in Delhi now produces high-quality fiber patch cord assemblies and accessories that meet global standards. Our founders — technically fertile minds with hands-on experience — believed that a company is only as creative as the people within it. That principle still drives every hire we make and every product we ship.

Timeline

How we've grown.

2019

Founded in Delhi

Abastel LLP incorporated with a focus on fiber optic products for FTTX and CATV networks. Manufacturing facility established.

2020

Expanding the Product Line

Extended into electrical wires and cables in collaboration with M/s Tortek India Private Limited. Now a complete end-to-end electrical supplier.

2021

Plastic Moulding & Machining

Launched precision plastic moulding and CNC machining capabilities — serving industrial and commercial customers across India.

2022

Packaging Solutions Vertical

Added industrial and consumer packaging as a dedicated vertical, completing our manufacturing ecosystem.

2023+

Aerospace Components

Entered the high-precision aerospace components space — our most demanding vertical, held to the tightest quality standards we've ever set.
